Ram Mandir

The Ram Mandir in Ayodhya is a revered Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Ram, located in Uttar Pradesh, India. It marks the birthplace of Lord Ram and symbolizes cultural and religious significance.

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is an ivory-white marble mausoleum located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh. It was commissioned by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

Qutub Minar

Located in Delhi, Qutub Minar is a 73-meter-high tower and is one of the tallest brick minarets in the world. It was built in 1193 by Qutb-ud-Din Aibak.

Red Fort

The Red Fort, located in the heart of Delhi, is a historic fort and a UNESCO World Heritage site. It was the main residence of the emperors of the Mughal dynasty for around 200 years.

India Gate

India Gate, located in New Delhi, is a war memorial built in honor of soldiers who died during World War I. It is a symbol of national pride and resilience.
